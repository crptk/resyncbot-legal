Privacy Policy for ResyncBot

Last Updated: August 7, 2025

What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy explains how ResyncBot handles information when you use our Discord bot service.

Information We DO NOT Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • User content (your videos, audio files, or media)
  • Discord messages or conversations
  • User profiles or personal preferences
  • Payment information (handled by secure payment processors)

Minimal Information We Do Collect

For technical operations only:

  • Command usage logs: Commands used and Discord user IDs (for debugging when things break)
  • Anonymous usage counts: How many commands used per day (for rate limiting)
  • Error logs: Technical error information when the bot malfunctions
Important: All this data is anonymous - we don't know who you are or link it to your Discord account.

How We Use This Information

  • Fix bugs: Command logs help us troubleshoot when the bot breaks
  • Enforce limits: Usage counts ensure fair access (15 random resyncs per day for free users)
  • Improve service: Error logs help us make the bot more reliable

We do NOT:

  • Analyze your behavior
  • Create user profiles
  • Share data with anyone
  • Use data for advertising
  • Sell any information

Data Storage and Deletion

  • Media files: Automatically deleted within minutes after processing
  • Debug logs: Kept for maximum 30 days, then automatically deleted
  • Usage counters: Reset every 24 hours
  • No permanent storage: We don't keep a database of users

Premium Subscriptions

  • Payment processing: Handled by secure third-party processors (we never see your payment info)
  • Subscription status: We only track if you have premium or free access
  • No billing data stored: All payment details handled by payment processor

Third-Party Services

We use these services:

  • Discord: For bot functionality (subject to Discord's Privacy Policy)
  • YouTube: For downloading videos (no user data shared with them)
  • Payment processor: For handling subscriptions (they have their own privacy policy)
  • Hosting provider: For running the bot (no access to user data)

Your Rights

Since we collect almost no data about you:

  • No data to access: We don't store personal information
  • Automatic deletion: All temporary data is deleted automatically
  • Contact us: Email resyncbot@gmail.com if you have privacy questions

Children's Privacy

Since we don't collect personal information, there are no special concerns about children using the bot.

International Users

Since we don't collect personal data, there are no international data transfer concerns.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

For privacy questions:

  • Email: resyncbot@gmail.com
  • Discord: onepathonly

Simple Summary

ResyncBot is designed to be privacy-friendly:

  • ✅ We don't collect personal information
  • ✅ Your media files are immediately deleted after processing
  • ✅ Only minimal technical data for debugging
  • ✅ No tracking, profiling, or data selling
  • ✅ Anonymous usage - we don't know who you are

Bottom line: We just process your videos and forget about them. That's it.
